About Me

Hello! I'm Kishor Neupane, a dedicated social worker from Nepal. As the Founder and President of KS Foundation, I am passionate about creating positive change through community initiatives, education support, health awareness, and empowerment programs.

My journey is driven by a commitment to uplift underprivileged communities and make a lasting impact on society.

KS Foundation

KS Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on social welfare, community development, and humanitarian efforts in Nepal. We work on initiatives that address education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and disaster relief.

Our mission: To build a stronger, more equitable society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Key Initiatives

  • Education Support
    Providing scholarships and resources to underprivileged students.
  • Health & Awareness Campaigns
    Organizing medical camps and awareness programs.
  • Community Empowerment
    Skill development workshops and women's empowerment programs.
  • Environmental Projects
    Tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns.
