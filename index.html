About Me

I am Kishor Neupane, Founder and President of KS Foundation, a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities in Nepal through education, health, and social welfare programs.

With a passion for creating lasting positive change, I lead initiatives that empower individuals and strengthen communities. KS Foundation focuses on sustainable development and direct impact on the lives of those in need.

Together, we build a better future for Nepal.
